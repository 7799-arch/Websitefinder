top 3 2000s shows

by laura and eleanor

gossip girl

Gossip Girl is a teen drama series focusing on privileged teenagers at a Manhattan private school whose scandalous lives are tracked by an unknown, omniscient blogger. Based on Cecily von Ziegesar's novels, the show centers on the return of "it girl" Serena van der Woodsen, her turbulent friendship with Blair Waldorf, and their group's scandals, romances, and social betrayals in the Upper East Side.click to watch gossip girl

the vampire diaries

Vampire diaries is about Vampire brothers, Stefan and Damon Salvatore, battle for the affection of selfless teenager, Elena Gilbert. Stefan is the good and kind-hearted brother that feeds on animal blood to keep from killing anybody, and Damon is the selfish and dangerous older brother that feeds on human blood carelessly. click to watch vampire diaries

gilmore girls

Gilmore Girls is a critically acclaimed comedy-drama (dramedy) focusing on the close, fast-talking relationship between Lorelai Gilmore and her daughter, Rory. Set in the quirky town of Stars Hollow, Connecticut, the series explores themes of family, love, and ambition across three generations as they navigate complex relationships, financial struggles, and personal growth. click to watch gilmore girls
