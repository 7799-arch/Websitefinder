Passacaglia

;
Passacaglia is a piece origionally played on the violin/cello, composed by Johan Halvorsen in 1894. The word "passacaglia" means "street song", from the Italian and Spanish word. It was also based on the final movement of George Frideric Handel's Harpsichord Suite in G minor. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GAIZxaToV2A
