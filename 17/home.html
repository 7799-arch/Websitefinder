My top artists

That you should to listen to!

Artic Monkeys

Artic momkeys, primarily an indie rock and alternative rock band. The Arctic Monkeys consist of four members Alex Turner, Jamie Cook, Matt Helders, Nick O'Malley. I love many Artic Monkeys songs such as why’d you only call me when you’re high, 505, fluorescent adolescent, i wanna be yours and many more mainly because of their groovy tunes!

A.R. Rahman

A.R. Rahman is an iconic Indian composer, singer, and music producer. He is celebrated for revolutionising Indian film music by blending Eastern classical melodies with electronic music, world genres, and traditional orchestral arrangements. Creating amazing songs such as all his Taal songs like Ishq Bina Ishq Bina, Taal Se Taal, Taal Se Taal Mila (Western) and many more which I enjoy for their great rythms and his other great songs like his Gahjini songs which are bangers.

Olivia Dean

Olivia Dean is an English singer-songwriter, who has rapidly ascended to global stardom through her soulful blend of pop, jazz, and R&B. With many great song such as Dangerously Easy, So Easy (To Fall in Love), Dive and many others that I love for the fun and upbeat vibe.

Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh is a celebrated Indian playback singer and music producer, widely regarded as the "voice of a generation" for his soulful and emotive vocal style. Whith many amazing songs like Naina one of my favourites with its emotional touch along side his other great songs.
