My top songs!

songs that I could listen to over and over again

Under Pressure-Queen & David Bowie

'Under Pressure' by Queen and David Bowie is one of those songs that you just can't hate. I take it to be a commentry on the pressures of life and the idea that you need to know exactly 'what it's all about' before the crippling pressure tears you apart. However, at the end of the song, it tells us to 'give love one more chance', perhaps hinting that the key to life is love and it is love that will break the cycle of pressure. David's clipped and minimalistic vocals contrasts with Freddie's extensive and bold vocal range, particularly at the end of the song during the final climax. Favourite line:'Cause love's such an old-fashioned word'

Gap Tooth Smile - Djo

Gap Tooth Smile by Djo is such an uplifting and fun song. I could listen to it 29 times and never get bored. This is a song of devotion to his love, as Djo says 'she's my number one' or 'she's my superstar', displaying his adoration for the girl with the gap-toothed smile. Towards the end of the song, he says 'God, I count my blessings from the one eyed dove'. A "dove's eye" is described as focusing on only one object at a time. In the context of this song, this means that Djo has "eyes only" for his partner, ignoring all other distractions. Overall, this song is one of my favourites, with the upbeat, beatles-inspired tune always improving my mood. Favourite line:'It's her hair, her style. But I fell in love with her gap-tooth smile'.

10:36 - Beabadoobee

10:36 by Beabadoobee is a fan favourite, and rightly so. The high-energy, indie-rock song is an earworm, but I invite ito getting stuck in my head. This song explores the dependancy on a 'warm body to hold' without an having or wanting an emotional connection to the person. In this song, Beabadoobee desribes the contrast between one partner wanting a deep romantic connection, while the other simply seeks comfort particularly emphasised in the line 'You don't need me As much as I need you'. My favourite line:'I know you thought it was just us, I didn't think you'd fall in love'
