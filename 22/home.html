Top 10 Friendliest Dog Breeds

In the whole world

Labrador

Labrador Retrievers are known for their loyalty. They are large dogs, with an energetic and sociable nature, perfect in family households.Colors range in three different options, black,brown and golden. Labradors originated in Canada and they are often used inside the police force, working with there hightened super senses!

Border Collie

Border Collie's are generally intellegent dogs. There intelligence fuels their calming nature. They are often used on farms or in working environments due to their obedience. They are easily trained,medium sized dogs, and flourish in most environments. Their thick coats protect them in winter, and on average can weigh 12-20kg.Border Collies are social dogs, they work well with other pets, specifically younger dogs to teach cooperation and obedience.RSPCA is Britains largest Animal focused Charity, they raise money to rescue animals to loving homes!!/

Poodle

Poodles, like Border Collies are smart dogs, however they are more well known for their lucious coat of fur.These dogs require regular grooming, and require mental and physical stimulation.
