Turtle

Turtles are reptiles with a shell developed mainly by their ribs and live in oceans around the world. Their are two groups of turtles Pluerodia (chelidae and african mud turtles) and Cryptodia (loggerhead,green and Kemps Ridley sea turtles) based on how their head retracts.Sea turtles have a broad diet and consume jellyfish,sponges,sea grass and more. They have been on earth for over 100 millon years and navigate the waters using earths magnetic field.Find out more and help by donating to turtle conservastion!!

In the wild they usually live up to 60-70 years olds and they live in herds.
