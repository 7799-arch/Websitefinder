spiderverse

spiderman

The original live-action Spider-Man was portrayed by Nicholas Hammond in the 1977 television film and subsequent series, while Tobey Maguire starred in Sam Raimi’s iconic 2002–2007 film trilogy. Earlier, a "mute" Spider-Man appeared in the 1970s The Electric Company sketches. The 2002 film is widely considered the first major cinematic, modern Spider-Man, grossing heavily and establishing the character in popular culture.

The multiverse

The Spider-Man Multiverse (often called the Spider-Verse) is a vast network of parallel universes where different versions of Spider-Man exist, all connected by a shared destiny known as the Web of Life and Destiny. This concept has been popularised through comic books, animated films, and live-action movies.There are 10 primary Spider-Man films featuring Peter Parker or Miles Morales as the lead, spanning three main live-action iterations (Tobey Maguire, Andrew Garfield, Tom Holland) and the animated Spider-Verse series. Including spin-offs in Sony's Spider-Man Universe (Venom, Morbius, etc.), the total expands beyond 15 movies.

creation

After being bitten by a radioactive spider—which also bit classmate Cindy Moon (Silk)—Peter gained superhuman strength, speed, and a precognitive "spider-sense". Despite his genius-level IQ (estimated at over 250), he frequently struggles with relatable "everyman" problems like paying rent and maintaining a secret identity from his boss, J. Jonah Jameson, at the Daily Bugle. Over his 60-year history, he has joined teams like the Avengers and Fantastic Four, worn various suits including the iconic black alien symbiote, and has even been voiced by celebrities like Chris Pine and Jake Johnson in animated adaptions.

combic(books)

Over the decades, Spider-Man has become the face of Marvel Comics through his flagship title, The Amazing Spider-Man, and various spin-offs like The Spectacular Spider-Man and Web of Spider-Man. His narrative is defined by the struggle to balance a chaotic superhero life with the mundane hardships of adulthood, including financial instability, college stress, and complex relationships with characters like Mary Jane Watson, Gwen Stacy, and the Black Cat.He has faced one of the most iconic "rogues' galleries" in fiction, including the Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, and Venom—the latter born from a sentient alien black suit Peter first wore in the 1980s.
