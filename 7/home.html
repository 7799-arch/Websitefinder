Top 3 animes

A list of the best anime shows I recommend watching

1.The Disastrous Life of Saiki K

The Disastrous Life of Saiki K. (Saiki Kusuo no Ψ-nan) is a popular Japanese gag comedy manga and anime series focusing on Kusuo Saiki, a high school student born with an overwhelming array of psychic abilities, including telepathy, telekinesis, teleportation, and X-ray vision. Despite his godlike powers, Kusuo desires only a normal, quiet life and tries to conceal his abilities from his classmates. However, while holding the power to protect the world, he is constantly surrounded by an eccentric group of friends who drag him into chaotic daily situations, making his life far from peaceful. 2. Death note

Death Note is a psychological thriller anime and manga series following Light Yagami, a brilliant high school student who finds a supernatural notebook dropped by the Shinigami (Death God) Ryuk. The "Death Note" kills anyone whose name is written in it while the user pictures their face. Light attempts to eradicate criminals to create a utopian world, becoming the infamous vigilante "Kira", while being hunted by an enigmatic detective known as L. 3.Spy × Family

Spy x Family follows a master spy codenamed Twilight who must create a fake family to infiltrate an elite private school and prevent a war. He unknowingly adopts a telepathic daughter, Anya, and enters a marriage of convenience with Yor, a deadly assassin. While the parents hide their secret identities from each other to maintain the peace, Anya uses her mind-reading powers to help keep their "pretend" household together, leading to a blend of high-stakes action and heartwarming domestic comedy.
